Welcome to the Durga Foundation Scholarship Program! We are dedicated to empowering deserving students by providing financial assistance to support their educational journey. Our scholarship aims to create opportunities for talented individuals who face financial constraints, enabling them to achieve their academic and career aspirations.





About the Scholarship:


The Durga Foundation Scholarship Program is designed to assist underprivileged and meritorious students in pursuing higher education. We believe that every student deserves an equal chance to succeed, regardless of their economic background. Through our scholarship, we offer monthly financial assistance ranging from 500 to 3000 INR, helping students cover their educational expenses and focus on their studies.



Eligibility Criteria:


– Completed high school and intermediate in 2023 from any board.
– Native residents of Uttar Pradesh.



Application Process:


To apply for the Durga Foundation Scholarship, eligible students need to complete our online application form. The form consists of personal information, educational background, and descriptive questions aimed at understanding the unique needs and aspirations of each applicant. We encourage students to provide detailed and thoughtful responses, as this will greatly assist us in evaluating their applications.



Selection Process:


The scholarship selection process involves two stages:

1. Merit-Based Shortlisting:

Applications will be reviewed based on academic merit, extracurricular involvement, leadership potential, and other relevant criteria. Shortlisted candidates will proceed to the next stage.


2. Interview Process:

Shortlisted students will be interviewed to assess their commitment, goals, and potential. This interaction allows us to gain deeper insights into the candidates’ aspirations and determine their suitability for the scholarship.



How to Apply:


The application period opens on 20 May and closes on 30 June 2023. Ensure that you submit your application before the deadline to be considered for the scholarship.
